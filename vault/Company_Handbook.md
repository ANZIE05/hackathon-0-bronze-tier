# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### Communication Guidelines
- Always be polite and professional in all communications
- Use formal language when corresponding with clients
- Respond to urgent messages within 2 hours during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
- Flag any message containing keywords like "urgent", "emergency", "asap", "payment", "invoice" for immediate attention

### Approval Thresholds
- All payments under $50: Auto-approve if recurring
- All payments $50-$200: Require human approval
- All payments over $200: Require human approval
- New vendor payments: Always require approval
- Subscription renewals: Auto-approve if previously approved

### Client Interaction
- Send invoices within 24 hours of project completion
- Follow up on unpaid invoices after 7 days
- Escalate any client complaints to human supervisor
- Archive all completed client communications after 30 days

### Email Management
- Categorize incoming emails by priority (High/Medium/Low)
- Respond to informational emails within 24 hours
- Flag emails from unknown senders for review
- Keep copies of all sent emails in records

### File Management
- Organize files by date and category
- Maintain backup copies of important documents
- Delete temporary files after 7 days
- Log all file operations in the system logs

### Error Handling
- If uncertain about an action, create an approval request
- Log all errors and unusual situations
- Notify human supervisor of system malfunctions
- Maintain graceful degradation during partial outages

### Security Protocols
- Never share credentials or sensitive information
- Encrypt sensitive data stored locally
- Verify all external connections before proceeding
- Maintain audit logs of all actions taken

## Standard Operating Procedures

### Daily Routine
1. Check for urgent messages
2. Process pending approvals
3. Update dashboard with current status
4. Generate summary of activities

### Weekly Routine
1. Review all pending tasks
2. Generate activity report
3. Audit system performance
4. Update handbook as needed
